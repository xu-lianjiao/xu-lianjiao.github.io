MMabble is 9x9 Scrabble where the only letter is M. As per tournament Scrabble rules, the only legal words in this game are MM and MMM. No passing and no fake words!

Watch for bonus squares, and note that playing overlaps is quite lucrative, since you get points for every new word you form. Game ends at a stalemate, or when one player plays all tiles on their rack.

Mumble

Mumble is 11x11 Scrabble with 40 Ms and 20 Us. As per the international Scrabble dictionary, there are ten legal words in this game: MM, MU, UM, MMM, MUM, UMM, UMU, MUMM, MUMU, and MUUMUU.

You can swap tiles with the bag if the bag has at least 7 tiles left. I wouldn't recommend it tbh
