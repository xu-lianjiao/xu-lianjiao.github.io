MMabble is an exciting 9x9 Scrabble variant that delivers fast-paced, strategic word-formation gameplay. Perfect for quick matches, the board features a curated layout of bonus squares designed to maximize scoring opportunities on a compact grid.

The pink starting square in the center offers a Double Word bonus to kickstart the game. From there, pathways of Double Letter (light blue) and Triple Letter (saturated blue) bonuses lead toward the outer edges, culminating in high-value Double Word (pink) and Triple Word (saturated red) multiplier squares at the corners.

Hover over any square for 1 second or click it to view its coordinate and score multiplier details in the tooltip.
